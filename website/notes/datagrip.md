Keyboard Shortcuts (for macOS)

- Toggle Database View: `⌘1`
- Refresh Database: `⌘R`
- Reformat Code: `⌥⌘L`
- Collapse All (Database Explorer): `⌘⇧–`
- Expand All (Database Explorer): `⌘⇧+`
