## Keyboard shortcuts
- New line in cell `⌘↩`
- Insert date `⌘;`
- Insert time `⇧⌘;`
- Insert date and time `⌥⇧⌘;`
- Insert row below `⌃⌥I` then `R` then `B`
## Notes
